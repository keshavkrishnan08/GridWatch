## Why this lands

You're not testifying for or against — you're handing them **a number about
their own proposal that they probably don't have**: what the grandfather clause
actually exempts, in megawatts, itemized.

That's the kind of thing a councillor's aide pulls out during debate, and it puts
your name and URL in a public record that reporters read.

## Two cautions

**This is a real public filing under your own name.** The route is IC 36-7-4-608,
which governs zoning ordinance amendments and is the basis for the
written-objections procedure the Council's own Journal recites. Confirm the
mechanics with the Clerk by phone before sending, and be comfortable with the
filing being permanent and public.

**Do not add a figure I haven't listed here.** The Google SE Marion 400 MW is
sourced only to AI Law Tracker, which is weaker than the rest, which is why it
sits in the table as withdrawn context rather than in the argument. One number a
councillor checks and finds wrong costs more than the whole submission gains.
