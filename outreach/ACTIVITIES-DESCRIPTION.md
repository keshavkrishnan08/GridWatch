## How to phrase it honestly

**Do:**
- "Sent to 12 Indiana organizations; adopted by 2"
- "Drove N public comments to state utility regulators"
- "Entered into the public record for an Indianapolis Council vote"
- "Engine verified on 3 regions; deployable to any state or country"

**Don't:**
- Cite **paid ad impressions** as reach. It reads as "I spent $50" and discounts
  everything after it. If you run ads, report *engaged actions*, never impressions.
- Say "used by" an organization that only received an email. Received ≠ adopted.
- Claim regions as "deployed" when they're auto-pulled scaffolds.

## The line to aim for

> "Adopted by [org], cited by [outlet], and entered into the public record for a
> city council vote — while remaining deployable to any region worldwide."

Every clause there is earnable in the next 90 days. None of it is true yet.
