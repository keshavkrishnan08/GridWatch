## Why this works: the precedent

Her July 6 story — *"Nearly a third of Indiana counties have moved to restrict
data centers"* — is built **entirely on IU's Environmental Resilience Institute
tracker.** She did not build her own tally. She used somebody else's dataset and
credited it. She did the same thing in June with United Way's ALICE data.

That is precisely the substitution you're offering.

## The opening: what she's missing

She has published **eleven data-center stories since October 2025. All are
siting and zoning. None quantifies ratepayer cost.**

That gap — not the map — is your pitch. Don't offer her a map. Offer her the
number nobody in Indiana has published: what this does to a household bill, by
county, with the filing behind it.

## Reach multiplier

Stories on `/statewide/` URLs run across **all nine Indiana Public Broadcasting
newsrooms.** WFYI also headquarters Side Effects Public Media and republishes
Indiana Capital Chronicle. One story here is not one story.
